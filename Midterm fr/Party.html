Trick or Treat Halloween
party 31 October 2023

Come and join us for an unforgettable Halloween Party filled with spooky fun and thrilling activities! We've planned three main activities that are sure to get your heart pumping and create lasting memories.

Adress: D7 Hanoi University, KM 9, Nguyen Trai Street.
Time: 6pm - 31/10/2023.

Activity 1: Haunted House Adventure
Enter our chilling haunted house and brace yourself for ghostly encounters and spine-tingling surprises. Navigate through dark corridors, eerie rooms, and encounter sinister creatures lurking at every turn. This nerve-wracking adventure will test your courage as you try to escape the clutches of the unknown.

Activity 2: Monster Mash Dance Party
Get your bones shaking and your feet tapping in our Monster Mash Dance Party! Join us on the dance floor as we boogie to Halloween-themed tunes and classic monster hits. Show off your best moves, unleash your inner ghoul or zombie, and dance the night away with fellow partygoers. Let loose and groove to the rhythm of the night in this spirited dance extravaganza.

Activity 3: Spooky Scavenger Hunt
Embark on a thrilling Spooky Scavenger Hunt and explore the hidden corners of our Halloween-decorated venue. Armed with clues and riddles, you'll venture into the unknown and solve perplexing challenges. Uncover hidden treasures, discover secret passages, and brave spine-chilling surprises along the way. The team that successfully completes the scavenger hunt in record time will be rewarded with a special treat and the admiration of all.

Remember to save your most bone-chilling or bewitching costume for our Halloween Cosplay Competition!
Dress up as your favorite character, whether it's a notorious villain or a legendary creature, and take center stage. Strut your stuff in the spotlight and impress our judges for a chance to win the coveted prize of a Special Trick Or Treat Candy Pack and the devilish sum of $666.

Join us for an unforgettable evening of hair-raising fun, from dancing to scavenging and cosplay. Embrace the Halloween spirit, let your imagination run wild, and revel in the excitement that awaits you at our Halloween Party. It's time to celebrate the spookiest night of the year in style!
